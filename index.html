Instructions

Welcome to Bubble Bobble, where you are Bob the friendly dinosaur. Your goal is to capture all of the Windup Toys in a bubble without running into them or letting them catch you. Once a windup toy has been caught, pop its bubble to transform it into a goody, which you can collect for points! You must pop all bubbles to move on to the next level.

Keys

  • A: move left
  • D: move right
  • W: jump
  • Spacebar: blow a bubble!
